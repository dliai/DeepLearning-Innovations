About DeepLearning Innovations (DLI AI)

Founded in 2024, we are an incorporated startup under Ministry of Corporate Affairs, recognized by Startup India, and accredited by AICRA. Our self-funded team of 5 permanent members is transforming communities through AI.

Our Mission

To empower communities through innovative AI-driven solutions that enhance education, healthcare, agriculture, and accessibility, fostering independence, inclusivity, and sustainable growth.

Our Vision

To become a global leader in AI innovation, transforming lives and industries by creating intelligent, affordable, and scalable technologies that address the unique challenges of underserved regions.

Core Technologies

Advanced algorithms for predictive analytics, computer vision, NLP, IoT smart sensors, Edge AI for offline functionality, AI-powered robotics, and big data platforms for regional datasets.

Our AI Solutions

We deliver comprehensive AI solutions tailored to meet the unique challenges of different industries, driving innovation and efficiency.

🎓

AI in Education

Personalized learning platforms with AI tutors and multilingual support for diverse learners. Target market: 250M students in India, 2M in J&K.

  • Online course: "Foundations of AI & ML" (Friday-Saturday classes)
  • Partnership with IntegratedLearning.us for Kashmir solutions
  • Adaptive learning platforms for diverse learners
  • Low-bandwidth solutions for rural schools
  • Multilingual support (Urdu, Kashmiri, Hindi)
🏥

AI in Healthcare

Mental health support, diagnostics, and blood donation networks. Target market: 600M rural population, 12M in J&K.

  • Mental Health Chatbot (NLP-based, with clinical psychologists)
  • AI-powered blood donation network (location, blood type matching)
  • Portable AI devices for TB, diabetes detection
  • Telemedicine for rural populations
  • 24/7 mental health support systems
🌾

AI in Agriculture

Precision farming tools for crop monitoring and yield optimization. Target market: 150M farmers in India, 2M in J&K.

  • IoT-based crop monitoring for saffron, apple, walnut farming
  • AI drones and sensors for smart irrigation
  • Predictive analytics for weather and market trends
  • Solutions tailored for J&K's hilly terrain and climate
  • Cost-effective using 3D printing and local resources
🤖

Assistive Technologies

AI-powered devices empowering differently-abled individuals. Target market: 15M visually impaired in India, 26M+ differently-abled.

  • BCI-enabled Robotic Glove for Rehabilitation (launching soon)
  • AI-guided smart glasses for visually impaired
  • Sign language translation gloves
  • Affordable, localized assistive solutions
  • Offline functionality for low-connectivity areas
